Duopel is an exclusive waterproof-breathable technology that is available as a finished fabric solution for sportswear, active wear, and lifestyle clothing brands.

Duopel is transforming the waterproof-breathable fabric space by giving designers a whole new range of design options.

Do more with color.

Duopel fabrics are available in every color, from classic black to neon pink.
Completely customizable, Duopel lets you create more with color.

Do more with graphics.

With Duopel fabrics, inner layers come to life with patterns and prints. Stripes, checks,
polka dots, and customizable graphics are just a few of the millions of possibilities.

Do more with textiles.

For a distinctive look and feel, Duopel fabrics are available in a wide range of
textiles, from synthetic to natural fabrics. Choose from our available lines, or
work with us to customize designs with the textile of your choice.

Duopel delivers more possibilities.

Duopel is a completely customizable waterproof- breathable fabric. In the Innovation Playground, we work closely together with our clients to create waterproof-breathable fabric solutions that best suit their personality, style, needs, and applications.
