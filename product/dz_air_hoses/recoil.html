Recoil

DingZing Air Hoses come in different sizes, structures, and burst pressures. Our premium air hoses are made of polyurethane resins and are widely used for air tools, pneumatic and hydraulic systems and machines, vacuum equipment, and oil and fuel lines.

Applications
  • • Air tools / oil and fuel lines
  • • Paint machines / hose reels
  • • Polyether-based PU hoses for water applications: gardening, cleaning, medical and hydration tube
  • • FDA and NSF grades available
 Features
  • • Light in weight
  • • High tensile strength and elasticity
  • • Excellent abrasion resistance
  • • Good resistance to fuel, oils, and chemicals
  • • Wide application temperature from -30ºC to +70ºC
  • • Durable and long-lasting
  • • Plasticizer free
Availability
  • • Burst Pressure: 25 bars
  • • Also available in inches with various lengths.
